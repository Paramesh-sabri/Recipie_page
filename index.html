Karaikudi Special Rasam

This rasam recipe has been cherished in our family for generations and continues to warm our hearts and souls.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup tamarind juice
  • 2 tsp rasam powder
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, chopped
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. In a pot, heat oil and add mustard seeds until they splutter.
  2. Add chopped tomatoes, turmeric powder, and salt. Cook until tomatoes soften.
  3. Pour in the tamarind juice and water. Bring to a boil.
  4. Stir in the rasam powder and let it simmer for 10-15 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.
